How To Evaluate Any AI Tool

The TIME Framework

⏱️

T — Time Saved

How many hours does this tool take off your plate each week?

🎯

I — Impact

Does it move a number that matters — revenue, response speed, quality?

🔧

M — Maintenance

What upkeep does it need — prompts, data, ongoing review?

🧩

E — Ease of Adoption

Can your team actually pick it up without a steep learning curve?

AI Best Practices & Policies for Your Business

Guardrails

Always keep a human reviewing customer-facing AI output.
Never paste confidential client data into a public AI tool.
Tell customers when they're talking to AI.
Pick one tool per task and standardize — don't let tools sprawl.
Write your AI rules down so new hires follow them too.
